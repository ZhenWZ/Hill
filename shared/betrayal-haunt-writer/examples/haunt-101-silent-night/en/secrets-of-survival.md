# Haunt 101 — Silent Night, Hungry Night
**Secrets of Survival**

> **Trigger Requirements** — Omens: Bite; Rooms: any omen room; Characters: —

*The scream from the next room dies into a wet snarl. Through the doorway you
see your friend's coat split along the spine, and what unfolds from it is tall
and gray and patient. The grandfather clock begins to chime the long count to
midnight. Your grandmother's stories were true after all — and they all agreed
on one thing: silver.*

## Right Now

Set aside 2 triangular tokens: these are the **Silver Weapon** tokens. The
traitor sets the Turn/Damage Track to 1; it advances at the end of each of the
traitor's turns. When it reaches 8, midnight strikes.

## What You Know About the Bad Guys

The traitor has become a werewolf and only needs to survive until midnight.

## You Win When ...

... the Werewolf is dead.

## How to Kill the Werewolf

Each hero can only attempt one step each turn.

1. **Find the silver.** In the Dining Room, the Chapel, or the Master Bedroom,
   attempt a Knowledge roll of 4+ to gather the household silver (candlesticks,
   bells, an heirloom crucifix). If none of these rooms is in the house, you
   may instead search in any room with an Item symbol. If you succeed, you are
   carrying the silver: place one of the set-aside triangular tokens face down
   on your explorer card. Face down or face up, the token is treated as an Item
   for dropping and picking up.

2. **Forge the weapon.** While carrying the silver, in the Kitchen or the
   Furnace Room (or, if neither is in the house, any basement room — the old
   heating pipes run scalding hot), attempt a Might roll of 4+ to melt and
   hammer it into a crude silver blade. If you succeed, flip the token face up:
   it is now a Silver Weapon token. It can be traded, dropped, and picked up
   like an Item.

3. **Draw blood.** Attack the Werewolf with Might while carrying a Silver
   Weapon token; when attacking the Werewolf this way, roll 2 additional dice
   (maximum 8). Each combat you win wounds it. Two wounds kill it (one wound,
   if there are 2 or fewer heroes).

The Werewolf is immune to Speed attacks. Heroes who attack it without silver
can stun it, but cannot harm it.

## If You Win ...

*The gray body steams in the cold hall, shrinking, until only your friend lies
there, breathing, human, with a silver blade beside them and snow melting on
the windowsill. Far away the clock finishes a chime that never reaches eight.
From the roof, faintly, you hear bells — sweet, and this time, right.*
