# Haunt 102 — Krampusnacht
**Traitor's Tome** · *Do not read until the Haunt begins.*

> **Trigger Requirements** — Omens: Madman; Rooms: any omen room; Characters: Zoe
> Ingstrom (dolls), if in play — see the heroes' setup
> **Traitor**: Haunt revealer

*The gibbering madman who has shadowed you all night finally lifts his head, and
the candlelight catches on horns you swear were not there before. The shape
unfolds — taller, blacker, dragging a sack that breathes. "You kept me company,
little friend," it purrs in a voice like cracking ice, "so you alone I will
spare. Help me fill the sack before the clock strikes twelve, and we shall ride
the long night down together." Outside, sleigh bells answer. The naughty are so
very many this year, and there is so little time.*

## Right Now

Your character is still in the game but has turned traitor. You control Krampus
on the monster turn. Replace the Madman token with the large **Krampus** monster
token in the room where the haunt was revealed, and put the **Sack** token
(pentagonal) in that same room — Krampus carries the Sack. Use the Krampus
traits printed at the end of this haunt.

You lose the Madman and any of its bonuses.

Set the Turn/Damage Track to 1 with a plastic clip. At the end of each of your
turns, advance the Track one number. When it reaches 8, midnight strikes.

## What You Know About the Heroes

They are trying to light the cold house room by room; if dawn's warmth fills
enough rooms, you are banished.

## You Win When ...

... the Turn/Damage Track reaches 8 and Krampus has not been banished — the long
night is yours and you drag your sackful of souls down to the cold below — or
else all heroes are captured or dead.

## The Krampus Must Do This ...

On the monster turn, Krampus moves up to its Speed and, once, must attack a hero
in its room if it can. Krampus carries the Sack and any captured heroes wherever
it goes; the Sack and captives do not affect its traits.

**Capture.** Krampus attacks with Might and deals physical damage. If Krampus
wins a combat by 2 or more, instead of dealing that damage it may stuff the
loser into the Sack: put that hero's figure on the Sack token. A captured hero
takes no turns, cannot be targeted, and cannot be the custodian of anything
until freed. If every hero is captured or dead at the same time, you win.

## Special Attack Rules

Krampus is immune to Speed attacks (the bells drown out gunfire). It cannot be
killed by ordinary means — heroes who defeat it in combat only stun it (flip the
token; it stands up at the end of the next monster turn) and may use that moment
to free a captive, but they cannot wound it. Only banishment ends Krampus.

On your own explorer's turn (not the monster turn), once, if you are in a Lit
room (one holding a face-up Warmth token), you may attempt a Might roll of 4+ to
snuff it: on a success, return that Warmth token to the heroes' supply and the
room is no longer Lit. *(With 3 or fewer players, the lights resist you: you
cannot snuff Lit rooms.)*

## If You Win ...

*The twelfth bell shudders through the rafters. The sack writhes, heavy and
warm, and the floor of the house yawns open onto a stairway of black ice. You
shoulder your harvest and begin the long climb down, bells ringing, while above
you a cold and lightless house forgets it ever knew the morning.*
