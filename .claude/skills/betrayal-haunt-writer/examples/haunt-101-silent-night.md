# Example 示例 — Haunt 101: Silent Night, Hungry Night / 平安夜，饥饿夜

> 演示一份**合格产出**：官方节次、双语、互斥胜利条件、有限收束、组件合法。
> 实际生成时三个部分各自独立成文件，放在 `haunts/haunt-101-silent-night/` 下。
> 触发 Trigger: **Bite 咬痕** × any omen room 任意预兆房间
> 拓展母题 Motifs: 狼人篇（变身/银器/月夜）+ 圣诞篇（午夜倒计时/炉火烟囱）
> 奸徒 Traitor: Haunt revealer 闹鬼揭示者

---

## Part 1 — Traitor's Tome 奸徒之书

*The bite on your arm has stopped bleeding, but under the skin something is
moving. Outside, snow muffles the world; far away you hear sleigh bells, sweet
and wrong. The grandfather clock begins to count toward midnight, and with
every chime the smell of your friends grows warmer, richer. You understand now.
Christmas morning will come, but not for them.*

*手臂上的咬痕已经止血，但皮肤之下有什么东西在游走。窗外大雪掩埋了整个世界；远处传来
雪橇铃声，甜美而诡异。落地钟开始敲向午夜，每一声钟响，朋友们身上的气味就变得更温暖、
更诱人。你终于明白了——圣诞节的清晨会到来，但他们等不到了。*

### Right Now / 此时此刻

Your explorer is still in the game but has turned traitor — and is transforming.
Drop all of your Items and Omens in your current room (put an Item Pile token
there). Replace your figure with a large round monster token: you are now the
**Werewolf**. Use the Werewolf's traits printed at the end of this haunt instead
of your explorer's traits.

Set the Turn/Damage Track to 1 with a plastic clip. At the end of each of your
turns, advance the Track one number. The Track is the clock striking toward
midnight (8).

你的探索者仍在游戏中，但已成为奸徒——而且正在变身。把你携带的所有道具与预兆放在当前
房间（放置一个道具堆标记），用大号圆形怪物标记替换你的人物模型：你现在是**狼人**。
使用本册末尾印出的狼人属性，不再使用原角色属性。

将回合/伤害轨用卡扣设为 1。你的每个回合结束时，将轨道推进一格。轨道即是敲向午夜
（8）的时钟。

### What You Know About the Heroes / 你对英雄的了解

They are scavenging the house for silver to forge a weapon that can kill you.
他们正在搜刮宅中的银器，想熔铸一件能杀死你的武器。

### You Win When ... / 当……你便获胜

... the Turn/Damage Track reaches 8 while the Werewolf is alive — the curse
becomes permanent and you vanish into the snowbound world — or else all heroes
are dead.

……回合/伤害轨到达 8 时狼人仍然存活——诅咒永久生效，你消失在茫茫雪夜之中——或者
所有英雄全部死亡。

### The Werewolf Must Do This ... / 狼人行动规则

You take a normal turn (there is no separate monster turn in this haunt). You
move like an explorer, up to your Speed. You can't carry or use Items or Omens,
can't be a companion's custodian, and can't make trait rolls except in combat.
Once during your turn, if you are in a room with a hero, you must attack.

You may enter the Coal Chute from the Basement Landing (the chimneys and flues
are yours tonight): doing so, you may emerge in the Kitchen, the Charred Room,
or any starting tile, if it is in the house. This counts as one space of
movement.

你进行正常回合（本剧本没有单独的怪物回合）。你像探索者一样移动，最多走等于速度的格
数。你不能携带或使用道具与预兆，不能担任同伴的监护人，除战斗外不能进行属性检定。
你的回合中，若与英雄同处一室，你必须攻击一次。

你可以从地下室平台进入煤滑道（今夜烟囱与烟道都属于你）：由此你可以出现在厨房、焦黑
房间或任意起始板块（若已在场上），计为 1 格移动。

### Special Attack Rules / 特殊攻击规则

You attack with Might and deal physical damage. If you inflict 2 or more points
of damage with one attack, the victim also takes 1 point of mental damage
(terror in the dark).

You are immune to Speed attacks (lead bullets pass through you). If you lose a
combat against a hero who is **not** carrying a Silver Weapon token, you take no
damage; instead you are stunned: flip your token, and skip your next turn. If
you lose a combat against a hero carrying a Silver Weapon token, you take a
wound: put one triangular token on this book. When there are 2 wound tokens
(1 if there are 2 or fewer heroes), the Werewolf dies and the heroes win.

你用力量攻击，造成物理伤害。单次攻击造成 2 点或更多伤害时，受害者额外受到 1 点精神
伤害（黑暗中的恐惧）。

你免疫速度攻击（铅弹只是穿身而过）。若你在战斗中败给**未**携带银武器标记的英雄，
你不受伤害，改为眩晕：翻转你的标记，跳过你的下个回合。若败给携带银武器标记的英雄，
你受到一处银创：在本册上放一个三角标记。当银创达到 2 个（英雄数不多于 2 时为 1 个），
狼人死亡，英雄获胜。

### If You Win ... / 若你获胜

*The eighth chime fades. Somewhere above, reindeer hooves scrape uselessly at
the roof of a silent house. You pad out into the snow, unhurried. A whole
sleeping town smells of gingerbread and warm blood, and the night is still
young.*

*第八声钟响散去。屋顶上，驯鹿的蹄子徒劳地刨着这座寂静宅邸。你不紧不慢地踱入雪中。
整座沉睡的小镇都散发着姜饼与温热血液的气味，而夜，才刚刚开始。*

**The Werewolf 狼人** — Speed 6 · Might 6 · Sanity 3

## Part 2 — Secrets of Survival 生存秘要

*The scream from the next room dies into a wet snarl. Through the doorway you
see your friend's coat split along the spine, and what unfolds from it is tall
and gray and patient. The grandfather clock begins to chime the long count to
midnight. Your grandmother's stories were true after all — and they all agreed
on one thing: silver.*

*隔壁房间的尖叫声化作一声湿漉漉的低吼。透过门缝，你看见朋友的外套沿着脊背裂开，
从中站起的东西高大、灰白、而且毫不着急。落地钟开始敲响通往午夜的漫长计数。
祖母讲的故事终究是真的——而所有故事都提到同一样东西：银。*

### Right Now / 此时此刻

Set aside 2 triangular tokens: these are the **Silver Weapon** tokens. The
traitor sets the Turn/Damage Track to 1; it advances at the end of each of the
traitor's turns. When it reaches 8, midnight strikes.

拨出 2 个三角标记作为**银武器**标记。奸徒将回合/伤害轨设为 1，并在其每个回合结束时
推进一格。轨道到达 8 时，午夜降临。

### What You Know About the Bad Guys / 你对坏家伙的了解

The traitor has become a werewolf and only needs to survive until midnight.
奸徒已变身为狼人，它只需要活到午夜。

### You Win When ... / 当……你便获胜

... the Werewolf is dead. ……狼人死亡。

### How to Kill the Werewolf / 如何杀死狼人

Each hero can only attempt one step each turn.
每名英雄每回合只能尝试其中一步。

1. **Find the silver. 寻找银器。** In the Dining Room, the Chapel, or the Master
   Bedroom, attempt a Knowledge roll of 4+ to gather the household silver
   (candlesticks, bells, an heirloom crucifix). If none of these rooms is in
   the house, you may instead search in any room with an Item symbol. If you
   succeed, you are carrying the silver: place one of the set-aside triangular
   tokens face down on your explorer card. Face down or face up, the token is
   treated as an Item for dropping and picking up.
   在餐厅、礼拜堂或主卧尝试知识检定 4+，搜集宅中银器（烛台、铃铛、传家十字架）。
   若这些房间都不在场上，可改在任意带道具符号的房间搜索。成功后你便携带着银器：
   将一个拨出的三角标记面朝下放在你的角色卡上。无论正反面，该标记在丢弃与捡起
   方面均视作道具。

2. **Forge the weapon. 熔铸银武。** While carrying the silver, in the Kitchen or
   the Furnace Room (or, if neither is in the house, any basement room — the
   old heating pipes run scalding hot), attempt a Might roll of 4+ to melt and
   hammer it into a crude silver blade. If you succeed, flip the token face up:
   it is now a Silver Weapon token. It can be traded, dropped, and picked up
   like an Item.
   携带银器时，在厨房或锅炉房（若两者都不在场上，则任意地下室房间——老旧的暖气管
   道滚烫）尝试力量检定 4+，将银器熔铸成一柄粗糙的银刃。成功后将该标记翻至正面：
   它现在是银武器标记，可像道具一样交易、丢弃、捡起。

3. **Draw blood. 见血封喉。** Attack the Werewolf with Might while carrying a
   Silver Weapon token; when attacking the Werewolf this way, roll 2 additional
   dice (maximum 8). Each combat you win wounds it. Two wounds kill it (one
   wound, if there are 2 or fewer heroes).
   携带银武器标记时用力量攻击狼人；以此方式攻击狼人时额外投 2 个骰子（上限 8 个）。
   每赢一次战斗即造成一处银创。两处银创杀死它（英雄数不多于 2 时只需一处）。

The Werewolf is immune to Speed attacks. Heroes who attack it without silver
can stun it, but cannot harm it.
狼人免疫速度攻击。没有银器的英雄攻击它只能将其眩晕，无法伤害它。

### If You Win ... / 若你获胜

*The gray body steams in the cold hall, shrinking, until only your friend lies
there, breathing, human, with a silver blade beside them and snow melting on
the windowsill. Far away the clock finishes a chime that never reaches eight.
From the roof, faintly, you hear bells — sweet, and this time, right.*

*灰色的躯体在冰冷的大厅里蒸腾着热气，渐渐缩小，最后只剩你们的朋友躺在那里——
还有呼吸，恢复了人形，身边是一柄银刃，窗台上的雪正在融化。远处，落地钟的报时永远
停在了第八声之前。屋顶上隐约传来铃声——甜美，而这一次，恰如其分。*

## Part 3 — Design Notes 设计说明（节选）

- **互斥与收束**：轨道到 8 且狼人存活 → 奸徒胜；狼人死亡 → 英雄立即胜；全英雄死亡 → 奸徒立即胜。三者覆盖所有终局，最迟第 8 个奸徒回合分出胜负。
- **任务链估算**：2 步检定（4+，成功率约 62%）+ 2 次加骰战斗（6v6，约五成），4 英雄 × 8 轮 ≈ 充分余量；3 人局降为 1 处银创补偿。
- **信息不对称**：英雄册不写狼人的烟道机动与恐惧附伤；奸徒册不写英雄寻银的具体房间与目标值。两册对时钟、银创数、免疫描述一致。
- **校验记录**：Hero ✅ PASS / Traitor ✅ PASS / Win-condition ✅ PASS（第 1 轮通过）。
